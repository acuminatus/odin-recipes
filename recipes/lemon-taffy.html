Lemon Taffy

Ingredients

  • 14 lbs. White Sugar.
  • 1/2 ounce Cream Tartar.
  • Saffron Coloring.
  • 2 quarts Water.
  • Lemon Flavoring.

Steps

  1. Proceed as directed for plain taffy.
  2. When the sugar reaches 305 degrees, add a few drops of saffron color.
  3. When it reaches 310 degrees, add a few drops of oil of lemon and pour out immediately into frames or tins; or if on pouring slab, mark out into bars or squares before it gets cold. The pouring slab should be level so that the sheet should be all the same thickness.
